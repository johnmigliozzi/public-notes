400g chicken thigh 2.25lb
7tbsp dark soy sauce
7tbsp honey
(1tsp corn starch + 1 tbsp water) slurry
lots of garlic
4 tbsp sriracha
100 ml chicken broth .85oz
1tbsp hoisin
1tbsp ginger

mix in slow cooker
cook on high 4-5 or low 7-8

add
200g noodles, cooked both packages
